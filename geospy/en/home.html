Discover the World with GeoSpy - Instantly Pinpoint Any Photo's Location


Ever stumbled upon a stunning photo and wondered where it was taken? Now, the answer is just a tap away! Whether it's a breathtaking landscape, a historical landmark, or a hidden gem, GeoSpy uses advanced image analysis to pinpoint the exact location of any photo you upload. Perfect for travelers, photographers, and curious minds, GeoSpy takes the guesswork out of photo exploration.


Simply upload a photo, and GeoSpy will utilize cutting-edge technology to determine its location—no EXIF data needed. Transform low-context photo data into precise GPS predictions. In seconds, you'll receive accurate GPS coordinates, a detailed map view, and even suggestions for nearby points of interest. It's like having a personal guide for every photo you discover.

Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How to start using GeoSpy?

After downloading and installing the app, you can simply click Upload photo to get the geolocation information of the photo. New users can try out some features for free.

How to subscribe?

You can choose weekly ($5.99/week) or yearly ($39.99/year) subscription in the app. After subscribing, you can enjoy unlimited conversations.

How to cancel subscription?

You can manage subscriptions in your iOS device settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions, find GeoSpy and manage or cancel your subscription.
